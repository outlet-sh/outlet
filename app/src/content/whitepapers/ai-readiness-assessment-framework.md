# AI Readiness Assessment Framework

## Know Where You Stand Before You Start

**What You'll Learn:**

### Introduction: Why Readiness Matters

- The cost of starting before you're ready
- Common readiness gaps that derail AI projects
- How to assess readiness in 2 weeks, not 6 months

### The 6 Dimensions of AI Readiness

#### 1. Strategic Alignment

- Do you have clear business objectives?
- Is leadership aligned on AI priorities?
- Have you identified high-value use cases?

**Assessment Tool:** Strategic Alignment Scorecard

#### 2. Data Foundation

- Is your data accessible?
- Is it clean and consistent?
- Do you have sufficient volume and quality?

**Assessment Tool:** Data Maturity Matrix

#### 3. Technical Capability

- Do you have the right infrastructure?
- Can your systems integrate with AI tools?
- Do you need custom development or commercial tools?

**Assessment Tool:** Technical Capability Checklist

#### 4. Organizational Readiness

- Does your team have AI literacy?
- Are your processes ready for AI?
- Is your culture open to automation?

**Assessment Tool:** Cultural Readiness Survey

#### 5. Governance & Risk Management

- Do you have AI policies in place?
- Have you identified potential risks?
- Do you have compliance requirements?

**Assessment Tool:** Governance Gap Analysis

#### 6. Change Management Capability

- Can you manage adoption and training?
- Do you have executive sponsorship?
- Have you addressed resistance?

**Assessment Tool:** Change Readiness Assessment

### How to Score Your Organization

**Scoring methodology:**

- Each dimension scored 1-5
- Weighted by impact and urgency
- Overall readiness score and interpretation

**Score Interpretation:**

- **25-30:** Ready to start immediately
- **18-24:** Ready with minor gaps to address
- **12-17:** Need foundational work first
- **6-11:** Significant preparation required

### Gap Prioritization Framework

Not all gaps are equal. Learn how to prioritize:

1. **Critical Blockers** (Fix first)
2. **Important Enablers** (Address soon)
3. **Nice to Have** (Improve over time)

### 90-Day Readiness Improvement Plan

**For each score range, we provide:**

- Specific actions to take
- Timeline and sequencing
- Resource requirements
- Success indicators

### Industry Benchmarks

How do you compare to others in your industry?

- Financial services readiness trends
- Manufacturing sector benchmarks
- Professional services norms
- Healthcare industry standards
